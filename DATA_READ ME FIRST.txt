READ ME FIRST

1- Data details the fate of Joannesia princeps seeds exposed to interaction with agoutis (Dasyprocta leporina)

2 -Data is on csv format

3- Each line represents a seed on a specific date

4- Columns entries are in portuguese language;
translation goes as following:

 Pontos - Location ID
 Nome - Name of Location
Data - Date of assessment
 Semente - Seed ID within location
 Detino - Seed Fate
   N= Intact
   P= Preyed
   D= Dispersed
   RP= Preyed after dispersal
   S= Missing
Enterrada - Seed Burrial
 S-Yes
 N-No
Distance - Distance from source
D2 - Distance of Secondary Dispersal
D3 - Distance of Tertiary Dispersal
D4 - Distance of Quaternary Dispersal
Mudanca - Is there any new information since last
check up?
 S- Yes
 N- NO
Sessenta- Number of independent records of agoutis in that location (Intensity of Space Use), corrected by trap effort
